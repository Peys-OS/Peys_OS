Frequently Asked Questions
How do I send USDC?
Type "send" followed by the amount and recipient's wallet address or phone number. Example: "send 10 0x1234..."
Is there a transaction limit?
Unverified accounts can send up to $500/day. Verified accounts can send up to $10,000/day.
How long do transfers take?
USDC transfers on Base are typically confirmed within seconds. Network congestion may cause delays.
I didn't receive my funds
Check your transaction history first. If the transaction shows as completed but you haven't received funds, contact support with your transaction hash.
