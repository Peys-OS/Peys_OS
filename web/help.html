Getting Started
How do I register? ▼
Send "register" to our WhatsApp bot, or tap the Register button. You'll need a 4-digit PIN to secure your account.
Is Peys free to use? ▼
Yes! Creating an account is free. A small 1.5% fee applies only when you send USDC.
What is USDC? ▼
USDC is a stablecoin pegged to the US Dollar. Unlike regular crypto, its value stays at $1.00, making it perfect for sending money.
Sending & Receiving
How do I send USDC? ▼
Use "send [amount] [recipient]" command. Recipients can be phone numbers, @usernames, or names.
How long do transfers take? ▼
Most transactions confirm within seconds on Base network. During high traffic, it may take a few minutes.
What's the minimum amount? ▼
Minimum transfer amount is 1 USDC. There's no maximum, but daily limits apply based on your verification level.
Security
Is my money safe? ▼
Your funds are secured by cryptographic encryption. Never share your PIN with anyone, including our support team.
What if I forget my PIN? ▼
For security reasons, we cannot recover your PIN. You'll need to create a new account with a different phone number.
