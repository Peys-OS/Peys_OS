Getting Started

How do I send USDC?
Go to Send, enter the recipient's wallet address, enter the amount, and confirm.
How do I receive USDC?
Go to Receive to see your wallet address. Share these with anyone who wants to send you USDC.
Is there a fee?
Sending USDC has a small network fee (gas) of around $0.01. There are no fees to receive funds.

Security

Is my wallet secure?
Yes, your wallet is protected by a PIN and all transactions require authentication.
What if I forget my PIN?
Contact support to verify your identity and reset your PIN.

Troubleshooting

Transaction failed
Check your balance and network connection. If the problem persists, try again later.
Funds not received
Transactions on Base are usually instant. If you still don't see funds, contact support.
