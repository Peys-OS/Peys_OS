# Peys Demo Video Script

## Video Overview

**Title:** Peys - Send Stablecoins via Email
**Duration:** 3-5 minutes
**Target Audience:** Hackathon judges, developers, crypto users

---

## Scene 1: Introduction (30 seconds)

**[VISUAL]**
- Peys logo animation
- Tagline: "Send crypto to anyone via email"

**[SCRIPT]**
> "Meet Peys - the stablecoin payment platform built on Polkadot. Send USDC, USDT, or PASS to anyone using email. No wallet required for recipients."

---

## Scene 2: Problem Statement (30 seconds)

**[VISUAL]**
- Show traditional wire transfer UI (slow, expensive)
- Show crypto wallet complexity (keys, gas, networks)

**[SCRIPT]**
> "Traditional payments are slow and expensive. Crypto payments require recipients to have wallets and understand blockchain. Peys solves this with Magic Claim Links."

---

## Scene 3: The Solution - Magic Claim Links (60 seconds)

**[VISUAL]**
- Screen recording: Connect wallet via Privy
- Select "Polkadot Asset Hub" network
- Enter amount: 10 PASS
- Enter recipient: john@example.com
- Click "Send Payment"
- Show transaction confirmation

**[SCRIPT]**
> "Here's how it works. Connect your wallet, select Polkadot Asset Hub, choose PASS token, enter the recipient's email, and confirm. That's it! The funds are locked in our escrow contract, and the recipient gets an email with a claim link."

---

## Scene 4: Recipient Experience (45 seconds)

**[VISUAL]**
- Show email received
- Click claim link
- Sign up with email (Privy)
- One-click claim
- Show funds in wallet

**[SCRIPT]**
> "The recipient doesn't need a wallet. They just click the link, sign up with email, and claim their funds. Privy automatically creates their wallet. They can now use or withdraw their stablecoins."

---

## Scene 5: Multi-Chain Support (30 seconds)

**[VISUAL]**
- Show network selector
- Switch to Base Sepolia
- Switch to Celo Alfajores

**[SCRIPT]**
> "Peys supports multiple chains - Polkadot Asset Hub, Base, and Celo. Each with near-zero transaction fees. We're bringing cross-chain payments next."

---

## Scene 6: AI Payment Assistant (30 seconds)

**[VISUAL]**
- Open AI chat
- Type: "Send 50 USDC to alice@example.com"
- Show AI extracting details and confirming

**[SCRIPT]**
> "Our AI assistant helps users create payments using natural language. Just tell it who to pay and how much."

---

## Scene 7: Technical Highlights (45 seconds)

**[VISUAL]**
- Show OpenZeppelin contract code
- Show escrow on block explorer
- Show transaction details

**[SCRIPT]**
> "Under the hood, we're using OpenZeppelin's Ownable and ReentrancyGuard for security. The PeysEscrow contract holds funds until claimed. Unclaimed payments auto-refund after 7 days."

---

## Scene 8: Closing (15 seconds)

**[VISUAL]**
- Peys logo
- Website: peys.app
- GitHub link
- "Built for the future of payments"

**[SCRIPT]**
> "Peys - making crypto payments accessible to everyone. Check us out at peys.app. Thank you!"

---

## Recording Tips

1. **Use Loom** or **OBS** for screen recording
2. **Set resolution** to 1920x1080
3. **Use a test wallet** with small amounts
4. **Practice the flow** before recording
5. **Add captions** for accessibility

## Required Assets

- [ ] Wallet with testnet tokens (PAS, USDC)
- [ ] Test email account
- [ ] Screen recording software
- [ ] Peys app running locally or on testnet URL

## Post-Production

1. Add intro/outro music
2. Add captions
3. Export as MP4
4. Upload to YouTube/Vimeo
5. Submit to hackathon
